[Exhibition] by Kagomoto Shinichi

籠本 真一

香川県の離島に在住する、籠本真一さんの人生初の展覧会を開催します。

２０１６年、大阪から志々島に移住し、２０１８年から「見た通りに描きたくなった」と絵画の制作をはじめました。
彼の志々島で作られた作品を、島での彼自身の生活とともに展示します。

We are going to held the exhibition of Shinichi Kagomoto who lives in a remote island around Kagawa pref and this is his first exhibition of his life.

He moved to the island from Osaka in 2016 and he has started drawing since 2018.
He thought he didn't want to draw as he sees.

You will be able to see his works with his life in Shishijima island in this exhibition.
